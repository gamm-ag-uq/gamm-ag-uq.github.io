Current Chairs

Andrea Barth

Andrea Barth studied mathematics and computer science in Mannheim. After earning her PhD on stochastic partial differential equations in Norway at the renowned Center of Mathematics for Applications at the University of Oslo, she spent four years researching and teaching at the Department of Applied Mathematics at ETH Zurich. In 2013, Barth initially became a junior professor and has since become a professor for "Computational methods for Uncertainty Quantification" at the Simulation Technology (SimTech) Cluster of Excellence at the University of Stuttgart.
